Privacy Policy for Seven Wonders Companion

Effective: 10 September 2025

Data Collection

The app does not collect personal information automatically. We may receive anonymous diagnostics and crash data (e.g., from Sentry) to improve stability.

User-Provided Data

If you choose to contact us from the feedback page, your email app opens and you decide what to send. We use your email address and message only to respond. We do not sell or share this information.

Children

The app can be used by all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please do not include personal information in emails to us. Parents or guardians who believe a child has sent us personal information may contact us to request deletion.

Data Retention

User-provided emails are kept only as long as needed to handle the inquiry, then deleted. Anonymous diagnostics may be retained for trend analysis and app reliability.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will update the effective date above and post changes on this page.
